Pokertron ♤ ♧ ♢ ♡

Utterly Simple Poker Evaluator

Nothing epic, just a minor mini-adventure in JavaScript.

About

Implements a crude library which evaluates the winner(s) among several 5 card poker hands. Note only a subset of the regular poker hands are getting handled:

  • Flush
  • Four of a Kind
  • Three of a Kind
  • Two of a Kind (Pair)
  • High Card

Input

Collection of players in the showdown.

  • Player Name
  • 5 Cards (each specifying the number and suit of the card)
  • Just follow the format shown in the example input below...
